NG WIZARD

Introduction

With visibility low and tension high, the slightest sound—a crunch of snow, a whisper of wind—could reveal a position or be a clever ploy. The map holds more than just snow and ice; hidden pathways, abandoned ruins, and mysterious relics add an ominous undertone, hinting at an ancient story lost to time. In this game of mystery and survival, one question looms: Who will remain, and who will vanish like a shadow in the snow?

Controls

Player 1: W A S D to move and Space to shoot

Player 2: Arrow keys to move and Enter to shoot
